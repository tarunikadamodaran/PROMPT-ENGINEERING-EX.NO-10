# CONTENT GENERATION SCENARIO 1

# ARTICLE ON CLIMATE CHANGE

## STEP 1 – BASIC PROMPT

```text
Write an article on climate change.
```

# FIRST DRAFT OUTPUT

Climate change refers to long-term changes in temperature and weather patterns caused mainly by human activities such as pollution and deforestation.

---

## STEP 2 – QUERY DECOMPOSITION PROMPT

```text
Write an article on climate change with introduction, causes, effects, prevention methods, and conclusion.
```

# REFINED OUTPUT

## Introduction

Climate change is one of the biggest environmental challenges affecting the world today.

## Causes

- Greenhouse gas emissions  
- Deforestation  
- Industrial pollution  
- Fossil fuel usage  

## Effects

- Global warming  
- Rising sea levels  
- Extreme weather conditions  
- Loss of biodiversity  

## Prevention Methods

- Renewable energy usage  
- Tree plantation  
- Reducing carbon emissions  
- Sustainable development  

## Conclusion

Global cooperation and environmental awareness are necessary to reduce climate change impacts.

---

## STEP 3 – SEMANTIC FILTER PROMPT

```text
Generate the article in an educational and formal tone suitable for college students.
```

# FINAL ARTICLE OUTPUT

A detailed educational article with formal structure, proper explanations, and environmental awareness content was generated successfully.

# CONTENT GENERATION SCENARIO 2

# CREATIVE WRITING – SCI-FI SHORT STORY

## STEP 1 – BASIC PROMPT

```text
Write a science fiction story.
```

# FIRST DRAFT OUTPUT

A scientist creates an advanced robot capable of traveling through time.

---

## STEP 2 – DECISION MAKING PROMPT

```text
Choose one futuristic setting and write a science fiction story involving Artificial Intelligence and space exploration.
```

# REFINED OUTPUT

The AI selected a Mars colony setting and created a story involving autonomous robots, advanced spacecraft, and human survival on another planet.

---

## STEP 3 – TAIL GENERATION PROMPT

```text
Extend the story with a conflict involving a malfunctioning AI system and a suspenseful ending.
```

# FINAL STORY OUTPUT

The story evolved into a detailed science fiction narrative featuring:
- Advanced AI systems  
- Space missions  
- Human-AI conflict  
- Suspenseful climax  
- Emotional ending  

---

# MENU ACTIONS PROMPT

```text
Choose one option:
1. Continue the story
2. Add a new character
3. Change the ending
4. Generate a sequel
```

# RESULT OF MENU ACTIONS

The AI selected “Generate a sequel” and extended the story with a new mission to another galaxy.
